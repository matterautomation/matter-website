SCADA Development

Matter Automation is provider of programming and system integration solutions for a wide range of SCADA applications, from straightforward remote I/O to complex process control for production plants and modern offices.

Our SCADA Design & Programming services include:

PLC Logic Programming: Our group of exceptionally skilled specialists and developers create PLC projects utilizing ladder logic, function block diagrams, structured text and instructional lists, and have adopted the leading international standards for PLC programming – which aims at reducing the complexity of programming different PLC models. Our professionals have great experience in PLC programming for industry leaders and giants including Siemens, GE, and Control Microsystems.

Our team specializes in developing customized SCADA solutions for clients in different industries.

Our SCADA designs provide following benefits:

  • Enable greater visibility to operators
  • Greater control over processes
  • Intelligent information systems
  • Increase plant safety and alarm notification
  • Give Top-Level Analytics of Production Data
  • Allow for Remote Support and Debug
