About Matter Automation

Matter Automation is a company designed to serve the Yukon through expert advice, great customer service and fair prices.

What Does Matter Automation Do?

Matter Automation installs and services automated systems. Everything from Building Controls to Water and Waste Water Processing Plants, Matter Automation is here to make your hardware work seamlessly together.

Providing services in Process Controls, Building Automation, HMI Graphics Development, SCADA Development, Data Cabling, Network Administration, Project Management or just plain old "I have a problem... help", we are here.

Efficient: Matter Automation programs systems to be as efficient as possible, eliminating wasted time, effort and energy from processes.

Customized: Matter Automation understands that your process is not like others. That there are no boiler plated procedures, and that is why processes and systems are designed to meet your specific needs. Matter Automation will work with persons from all levels to make sure that the system being designed provides each level with exactly what they need.

Integration: Matter Automation knows there is a 100 different ways to accomplish a task and a 100 components to provide you the ability to accomplish that task. You should not be limited to only the most expensive components, that is why Matter Automation works hard at connecting all of your components together no matter what the name. If connection is not possible, Matter Automation will suggest the most cost effective options.

Partnerships: One company can't do it all, that is why Matter Automation has created partnerships with local and outside experts. We let the electrical and mechanical experts be the experts while we focus on our expertise: getting your system working.
